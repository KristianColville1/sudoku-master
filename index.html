How to play sudoku

To finish the game, each row, column, and 3x3 grid must contain the numbers 1 through 9.

The goal is to ensure that no conflicts exist in each row, column, and 3x3 grid.

Each row, column, and 3x3 grid has nine cells.

There are six different difficulties to choose from in this game; if you are a beginner, we recommend starting with Very Easy.

This game has 11 fill-in-the-blank options, the numbers 1-9, an eraser, and a pencil.

When there is no immediate answer, use the pencil to help you keep track of your potential guesses for cells.
